The game

What is it about?

This games focuses around eating healthy. We set our goal to teach kids at age 5-10 which foods are healthy and which arent't. Find out how to play below :)

How to play

This game is played with up to 10 players. The game has 5 rounds in total and the person who scorse 10 points wins.

Each round one player gets shown two images of different food items. They have to decide which one is the healthy option.

During the players turn they need to figure out which option is more healthy.

If the player selects the correct answer, they receive 2 points and the the next player has to find the healthy food option.

If the player selects the wrong answer, they receive 0 points and an explanation which grocery/food item is healthier. From there the next player takes a turn at finding the healthy option.

As soon as one player has received 10 points they win the game.
